I am a Machine Learning and Software Engineer with a deep interest in **Computer Vision**, **3D Graphics**, and **Neural Rendering**. Currently, I am based at the University of Fribourg, where I work on bridging the gap between deep learning and classical graphics techniques.

My technical expertise spans implementing cutting-edge research like **3D Gaussian Splatting** to developing AI-powered creative tools and exploring the security implications of **Federated Learning**.

Research Interests
======
* **Neural Radiance Fields & 3DGS:** Real-time radiance field rendering and 3D reconstruction from sparse viewpoints.
* **AI Security:** Investigating data extraction and "stealing" attacks within distributed and federated learning environments.
* **Computational Geometry:** Numerical solvers (FEM) for physical simulations on complex triangulated surfaces.
* **Optical Music Recognition (OMR):** Applying CV to automate music transcription and interactive practice tools.

Featured Projects
======
### [3DGS from Scratch](https://github.com/ChungAndre/3DGS-from-Scratch)
A "from-scratch" implementation of 3D Gaussian Splatting, focusing on high-fidelity real-time rendering and understanding the underlying optimization process.

### [Holoframe](https://github.com/ChungAndre/Holoframe)
A Python tool that leverages AI-powered monocular depth estimation to transform standard 2D video into 2.5D holographic effects.

### [TB-OMR: Bachelor's Thesis](https://github.com/ChungAndre/TB-OMR)
Developed a system for Optical Music Recognition, exploring the intersection of computer vision and musicology to digitize sheet music accurately.

### [Ginzburg-Landau FEM Solver](https://github.com/ChungAndre/ginzburg-landau-fem)
A C++ based Finite Element Method solver for Ginzburg-Landau equations, specifically designed for triangulated 2D surfaces.
